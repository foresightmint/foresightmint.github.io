FAQ

Common questions.

You need Power BI Desktop (free) to open and use the report with your own data. Power BI Pro or Premium is only required if you want to publish and share the report within your organization via the Power BI Service.
The MVD is designed for Excel. Power BI can connect to Google Sheets, but requires some configuration. The Getting Started guide covers the steps to make this work. The core structure is identical — it's a straightforward import.
The data model is a standard star schema — it scales to hundreds of projects limited only by your machine's memory and Power BI's data model capacity. The demo ships with 3 projects. At 50 projects, Risk Radar becomes a true early warning system.
The MVD is designed to be adapted. Each field is documented in the README tab. If your source data has different field names or structures, the Getting Started guide explains how to map your data to the model. Most organizations can adapt in under an hour.
One-time purchase. You own the files. No recurring fees, no account required after download, no lock-in. If we release major updates, we'll announce them — but V1 is complete and fully functional as delivered.
Every project has a Reported RYG (what the PM entered in their status report) and a Computed RYG (what the data says based on 21 weighted signals). If a project reports Green but the signals produce a Red score, Risk Radar flags it as UNSUPPORTED. That's a status mismatch — and that's what leadership needs to know about.
