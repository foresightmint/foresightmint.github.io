Flow Capability Chart

A free Power BI custom visual by ForesightMint.

About

The Flow Capability Chart is a frequency distribution with a cumulative percentage overlay, paired with rich filtering. The bars show the shape of the work. The cumulative line shows where the long tail begins. The filters get the team from shape to cause in seconds. It is the visual companion to the methodology in Building the Enterprise in Flow, designed to drive guided retrospectives.

Drop in a numeric fact and a unique row identifier. No DAX, no bin creation, no setup. Tinker from there.
