Flow Capability Chart

A Power BI custom visual by ForesightMint.

About

Flow Capability Chart is a sorted-bin distribution chart with a cumulative percentage line — a "histogram-Pareto" — designed for retrospective and quality-review analysis. Drop in a numeric field and a row identifier, and the visual answers the question: what is your process capable of right now?
