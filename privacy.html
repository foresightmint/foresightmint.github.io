The Flow Capability Chart ("the visual") performs all data processing locally within your Power BI environment. Any data the visual displays remains entirely within your Power BI report and under your control.

Because the visual sends no information to ForesightMint or any third party, ForesightMint does not receive, retain, or have access to any data you use with the visual.
